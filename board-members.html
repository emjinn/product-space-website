Linda Wu

President

As a CS major, I had no clue what product was but I knew I couldn’t be an engineer and code for the rest of my life. As someone who values getting to work with people and forming connections, I always wanted to understand what Product Management was and how it combined my passions for business and tech. Product Space provided the mentorship and community I needed, giving me a safe space to grow and ask questions while introducing me to a network of talented individuals. As a Product Management fellow in 2021-22, I was able to work in a team as a PM and collaborate with design and marketing to make a cool capstone project.

I hope to give back to this fellowship by fostering an environment where fellows can learn from each other and grow together while deepening our understanding of product. Let’s learn together!

Nhathan Nguyen

Vice President

In 2020, I entered college as a human biology major on a pre-med track. As a first-generation college student, I didn’t know what product or tech was or much less that it existed. Throughout my first year of college, I was exposed to product and became intrigued by the idea of pursuing a related or adjacent career in it. As a previous Product Management fellow, I was able to use data, analytical problem solving, and collaborative teamwork to solve business problems. This hands-on experience solidified my passion for product - this is what I wanted to do and the impact I want to make for the rest of my life.

I joined Product Space board in hopes of returning this experience to someone else. We, first and foremost, are a safe space for people to learn about the product world. Regardless of your year, major, and experience, we welcome everyone to come to our recruitment and see if our community is a right fit for you.
